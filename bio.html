A short bio

Emily grew up in Louisville, KY before attending Dartmouth, where she studied Computer Science, ran the Dartmouth Women in Computer Science club, and swam competitively on the Women's Varsity Swim Team. She currently works as a Lead Software Engineer at The MITRE Corporation, where she leads and contributes to projects focusing on the application of novel Machine Learning research, particularly in the field of Computer Vision, to new domains. She received a Master's of Computer Science degree at Tufts University in 2021. Emily is currently a Computer Science PhD Candidate at Tufts, where she focuses on applying novel data science and machine learning research to evaluating climate policies. Outside of work and school, Emily can be found working through different fermentation projects, cultivating her garden, and reading mysteries and thrillers.
