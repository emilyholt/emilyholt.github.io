A short bio

Emily grew up in Louisville, KY before attending Dartmouth, where she studied Computer Science, ran the Dartmouth Women in Computer Science club, and swam competitively on the Women's Varsity Swim Team. She currently works as a Senior Cyber Security Software Engineer at The MITRE Corporation, where she leads and contributes to projects focusing on the application of Machine Learning to Cyber domains, and the application of Cyber techniques to the field of Machine Learning. She is currently pursuing a Master's of Computer Science degree at Tufts University. Outside of work and school, Emily can be found working through different fermentation projects, baking bread, and playing in the garden, learning how to cultivate and not kill off all her seedlings.
